balance_leaf - reiserfs tree balancing algorithm : tree balance state : item header of inserted item (little endian) : body of inserted item or bytes to paste : i - insert, d - delete, c - cut, p - paste (see do_balance) passed back: : key to insert new nodes : array of nodes to insert at the next level

In our processing of one level we sometimes determine what must be inserted into the next higher level. This insertion consists of a key or two keys and their corresponding pointers.
